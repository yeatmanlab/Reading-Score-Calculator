Literacy Test Raw Score Convertor

Double check your input .csv before generating output scores!

This is a web application that can calculate scores from various reading assessments based on raw input .csv files.

The following assessments are supported:

  • TOWRE SWE
  • TOWRE PDE
  • CTOPP MFD
  • CTOPP RDN
  • CTOPP RLN

Scoring App Reminder

Before you process the input data, please enter the participant's age in years and months at the time of testing. This information can be found in REDCap when you prep the run sheet.

For example, if the participant is 11 years and 0 months old at the time of testing then enter "11" in Age (Years) and "0" in Age (Months). It's important to enter the participant's age at the time of testing because it's used to calculate their scores!
